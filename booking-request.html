What is the procedure before and after I arrive in Thailand?

At the time of payment for your accommodation, Mediseekers will book all hospital, clinic appointments (as the case may be) and hotel accommodation and transfers.

During your stay in Thailand, you will be assisted by a representative from Mediseekers associate company in Thailand, who will provide the pick-up and transfer at the airport at a prearranged time.
On the day following your arrival you will have an appointment with the nominated Surgeon for a pre-operative consultation (Surgeon Consultation).

Within 48 hours of the Surgeon Consultation and provided that the Surgeon and you agree to proceed with the Elective Surgery Procedures, you will be admitted to the Hospital for the purpose of undergoing the Elective Surgery Procedure. 
Following the Elective Surgery Procedure, you will remain in the Hospital for an observation period and post operative consultations as required by the Surgeon.

At an appropriate time (as determined by the Surgeon), you will be discharged from the Hospital and return to your hotel.  The Surgeon will recommend a period of time that you should remain in Thailand for recuperation.
Mediseekers will use their best endeavours to ensure that the representative from Mediseekers associate company in Thailand is contactable 24 hours a day during the time you are in Thailand. If you would like to add, change or cancel any treatments, please give at least 48 hours notice to the representative.

This process described above may vary from patient to patient.

What is the purpose of the Surgeon Consultation?

The Surgeon Consultation serves a number of purposes.  These include the following.

  • The Surgeon Consultation will help the Surgeon determine the appropriateness (from a medical perspective) of each Elective Surgery Procedure you wish to undergo. 
  • The Surgeon will take the opportunity to explain the surgery and the procedure. The Surgeon will also inform you of any form or consent which the hospital may require from you or to be signed by you..
  • To give you the opportunity to meet the Surgeon and ask any questions you may have.
  • To give you the opportunity to decide and confirm whether to go through with the Elective Surgery Procedure.
  • In addition, the Surgeon will determine if any additional procedures are required that will add to the cost as set out in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate.

Both you and the Surgeon are under no obligation to proceed with an Elective Surgery Procedure at any time.

How will Mediseekers advise me of the price for Elective Surgery Procedures?

The price for Elective Surgery Procedures is determined in two stages:

Stage 1: Based on the information provided by you to Mediseekers, we will make enquiries with third parties for the purpose of giving you an estimate of the price for Hotel Services and Elective Surgery Procedures.  This estimate is herein referred to as the "Pre-Consultation Price Estimate" (the price for Hotel Services and Elective Surgery Procedures will be separately itemised).

Stage 2:  Following your arrival in Thailand, you will be required to attend the Surgeon Consultation.  Following the Surgeon Consultation, the final price for Elective Surgery Procedures will be determined, (the "Final Price Amount").

When the Final Price Amount is decided and agreed by you, you will pay the amount directly to the hospital in accordance with the terms of payment of the hospital before surgery.

What goods and services are included in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate?

The Pre-Consultation Price Estimate will include each of the services specified below:

(a) Hotel Services: The estimated price for Hotel Services, which may include (subject to your required package) the following:

  • Accommodation at a specified hotel;
  • Transfer from Bangkok or Phuket airport to your hotel and return to the same airport;
  • Transfers from your hotel to the hospital and return to the hotel for the initial consultation, the elective procedure and at least two further visits to the hospital for post operative attention; and
  • Meals (if you elect for meals to be included in your package).

(b) Elective Surgery Service: The estimated price of each Elective Surgery Procedure and the related On Cost (as defined below) for that particular procedure.

Important note:  You acknowledge and accept that the estimated price of each Elective Surgery Procedure is only an estimate and is subject to changes or variations after the Surgeon Consultation. 
Unless otherwise specified in your Pre-Consultation Price Estimate, the "On Costs" for each Elective Surgery Procedure includes:

  • One Surgeon Consultation;
  • The services of the Surgeon for the purpose of carrying out the selected a Elective Surgery Procedure(s);
  • The services of a specialist anaesthesiologist (if required);
  • Post operative accommodation at the private clinic/hospital for the specified period of time for the required procedure;
  • All theatre charges for carrying out the required procedure;
  • General medication required immediately following post operation,
  • Post-operative consultation prior to your departure from Thailand.

It is not possible to determine the exact price of Elective Surgery Procedures without first having the Surgeon Consultation (as discussed above).

What goods and services are excluded in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Quote?

The following goods and services are excluded from the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Amount:

  • Any additional medical expenses that might be incurred following any Elective Surgery Procedure as a result of unforeseen complications or as may be included in the Final Price Amount.
  • Travel insurance and / or any medical insurance (see discussion below).
  • Any procedure for which an estimate is not given in respect of in Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Quote.
  • Tours or outings.
  • Any time spent at a hotel in excess of the departure date specified in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Amount.
  • Additional expenses not specified in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Amount.
  • Meals (unless they are specified in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Amount as being included in the price).
  • Air tickets (or other transport) to/from Thailand.
  • Airport or departure taxes, road tolls, laundry, beverages, gratuities or tips.
  • Additional costs incurred by weather or failure of transport services or other circumstances beyond the control of Mediseekers.

How long is the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate valid for?

The Pre-Consultation Price Estimate is valid for 21 days from the date on which it is given to you, or an earlier date if advised by Mediseekers.

Can the final price vary from the price in the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and the Final Price Quote?

Yes. For example:

  • Pre-Consultation Price Estimates are expressed in a specified currency and reflect anticipated exchange rates unless otherwise indicated. Prices are subject to review (and change) as Mediseekers, the relevant hospital or hotel may advise.
  • As discussed above, the Elective Surgery Procedures may vary following the Surgeon Consultation.
  • All credit card payments are subject to exchange rate fluctuations, determined by your issuing bank.

What are the terms of payment?
If you wish to use the Hotel Booking Service and Elective Surgery Booking Service, the following procedure applies:

  • Confirmation of Pre-Consultation Price Estimate: Mediseekers will confirm that the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate is correct, that prices for Hotel Services and Elective Surgery Procedures have not varied and that your requested dates remain available.
  • Pay for Hotel Services and any flights: After Mediseekers gives you confirmation of the Pre-Consultation Price Estimate and confirms availability of requested dates, then you can book the Hotel Services and Elective Surgery Procedures as set out in the confirmed Pre-Consultation Price Estimate by paying for a sum as may be specified by Mediseekers [the total booking price excluding the surgery costs]. See the Important Terms below regarding the non-refundable conditions attached to the booking.

Important term: You acknowledge and agree that the booking is non-refundable to you in any circumstances.

What happens if after the Surgeon Consultation, I decide not to proceed with any surgery or the Surgeon refuses to perform a particular procedure?

If, following the Surgeon Consultation, the Surgeon or yourself elects not to proceed with the Elective Surgery Procedure, none of that amount paid for the Hotel Services will be refundable to you.

What happens if I wish to vary my booking?

Following the payment of your accommodation, you may amend your dates of travel, subject to any administration fees and any surcharges or fees imposed by the hotel in respect thereof. But, note that the booking is non-refundable and the accommodation elements may not be cancelled.

Mediseekers is not a travel agent and does not provide advice in relation to visa or passport requirements for Thailand.
It is your responsibility to make sure all required travel documents when entering Thailand are valid and correct.

Do I need to take out my own travel and medical insurance?

Yes.  Mediseekers does not include any insurance in the cost of the Hotel Services or Elective Surgery Procedures.

Accordingly, Mediseekers strongly recommends that you take out travel insurance, so that you will be comprehensively insured for your travel. Subject to your requirements, Mediseekers recommends that you take out travel insurance that covers you against cancellation fees and loss of deposits, emergency medical, overseas medical and hospital expenses, theft, luggage delay, travel delay, personal liability and car rental excess.

Certain travel insurance may not cover you for costs incurred as a result of elective surgery or medical malpractice in connection with elective surgery.  Mediseekers recommends that you consider obtaining specialist insurance for this purpose. 

Mediseekers strongly recommends that you disclose all relevant matters to your insurer (including the fact that you may be undertaking elective surgery as part of your travel).

Important term:  You acknowledge that Mediseekers has not offered you any insurance or advice in relation to a particular insurance product.  You have reviewed the terms of your insurance policy (if any) and are satisfied that it provides you with the level of insurance you consider that you require.

Is a surcharge payable if I use a credit card to pay for my booking?
Yes.  Mediseekers will charge you an amount equal to 1.75% of the cost of your booking if you use a credit card to pay.
